Steak Recipe

Description

This steak is perfect for those who are on the carnivore diet as there are no extras at all in this recipe. All you need is your steak, cooking fat, and salt!

Ensure that you complete the reverse sear as it will really lock in those delicious juices, and maintain extra tender bites. Following these directions will ensure you don't miss marinades or extra pointless seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Cooking fat (Beef Tallow or Butter)
  • Redmond Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 250 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Put your raw steak on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and salt generously.
  3. Insert your thermometer. Monitor the temperature and let your steak cook until it reaches your level desired level of doneness. Because you are going to sear the steak after and the temperature will rise considerably, take take it out of oven at around these temps below.
    • Rare / Blue Rare: Remove steak at 100-105 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Medium Rare: Remove steak at 115-120 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Medium: Remove steak at 130-135 degrees Fahrenheit
  4. Heat up your cast iron or stainless steel pain on the stovetop on med-high, then add in enough beef tallow to coat the pan. Once the pan is very hot, add your steak(s) in and sear each side for about 1.5-2 mins.
  5. from the pan and let rest for 10 minutes. Enjoy!
  6. (optional) Add butter or roasted bone marrow and extra salt to taste. You won't regret it.
