Carnivore Bacon and Eggs Recipe

One of the easiest breakfasts to have each morning that never gets old. You can always choose how to cook your eggs, but I prefer mine sunny-side-up!

A simple recipe with only 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 4 large free range eggs
  • 4 rashers of free range bacon
  • Himalayan salt & cracked black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet to a medium high heat.
  2. Cook the rashers of bacon to your desired level of crispness, for around 3-4 minutes per side
  3. Remove the bacon, leaving the bacon fat in the pan and turn the heat down to medium/low
  4. Crack each egg and carefully place into the pan
  5. Let the egg whites turn white while ensuring eggs do not stick to the pan
  6. I like to flip my eggs for about 30 seconds
  7. Add to plate with bacon and enjoy!
